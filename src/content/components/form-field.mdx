## Usage

```html
<cs-form-field label="Name" description="Your full name.">
  <cs-input placeholder="John Doe"></cs-input>
</cs-form-field>

<cs-form-field label="Email" error-text="Invalid email.">
  <cs-input type="email"></cs-input>
</cs-form-field>
```
