```html

  <div>Item one</div>
  <div>Item two</div>
</cs-space-between>

<cs-space-between direction="horizontal" size="s">
  <cs-badge color="blue">A</cs-badge>
  <cs-badge color="green">B</cs-badge>
</cs-space-between>
```
